Meghalaya Cuisine

Meghalaya cuisine is known for its use of local ingredients and unique cooking methods, focusing heavily on meat and rice dishes.

Popular Dishes:

  • Jadoh

  • Dohneiiong (Pork with Black Sesame)

  • Khar

  • Shnut Pulao

  • Pukhlein

  • Chicken with Black Sesame Seeds

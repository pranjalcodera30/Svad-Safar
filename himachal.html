Himachali Cuisine

Himachali cuisine is known for its delicious flavors and the use of local ingredients. Dishes are often prepared with a variety of spices, lentils, and grains.

Popular Dishes:

  • Sidu

  • Chana Madra

  • Dham

  • Bhaturu

  • Patande

  • Kullu Trout

  • Aloo Palda

  • Chowmein

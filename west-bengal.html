Bengali Cuisine

Bengali cuisine is known for its subtle flavors, vibrant colors, and emphasis on fish and rice. It beautifully combines different spices to create unique tastes.

Popular Dishes:

  • Fish Curry(Shorshe Ilish)

  • Rosogolla

  • Puchka (Pani Puri)

  • Chingri Malai Curry

  • Sandesh

  • Bengali Mutton Curry

  • Shorshe Bata Maach

  • Aloo Posto

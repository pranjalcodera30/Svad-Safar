Jharkhandi Cuisine

Jharkhand's cuisine is diverse and often incorporates locally sourced ingredients. It features both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes, often prepared with minimal spices.

Popular Dishes:

  • Dal Pitha

  • Rugda

  • Patla Bhaji

  • Khichdi

  • Gond ke Laddoo

  • Fish Curry

  • Chura (Beaten Rice)

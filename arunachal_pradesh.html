Arunachal Pradesh Cuisine

Arunachal Pradesh cuisine features a mix of Tibetan and Indian influences, focusing on rice, meat, and vegetables, with minimal spices.

Popular Dishes:

  • Thukpa

  • Momoko

  • Pork with Bamboo Shoot

  • Apong (Rice Beer)

  • Gaba Rice

  • Bhut Jolokia Pickle

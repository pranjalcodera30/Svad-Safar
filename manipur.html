Manipuri Cuisine

Manipuri cuisine is characterized by its use of fresh ingredients and minimal oil. It is known for its unique flavors and is often less spicy compared to other Indian cuisines.

Popular Dishes:

  • Manipuri Fish Curry

  • Singju

  • Chakhao Kheer

  • Morok Metpa

  • Wangoi

  • Eromba

  • Kangshoi

  • Chakhao

Rajasthani Cuisine

Rajasthani cuisine is known for its rich flavors and vibrant colors, reflecting the culture and traditions of the region. The food is often made with locally sourced ingredients and is renowned for its use of spices and ghee.

Popular Dishes:

  • Dal Baati Churma

  • Gatte ki Sabzi

  • Ker Sangri

  • Laal Maas

  • Rajasthani Ghevar

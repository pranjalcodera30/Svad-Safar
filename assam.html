Assamese Cuisine

Assamese cuisine is known for its use of fresh herbs, vegetables, and fish. The flavors are generally mild but are enhanced by unique cooking techniques.

Popular Dishes:

  • Pitha

  • Khar

  • Masor Tenga

  • Duck Meat Curry

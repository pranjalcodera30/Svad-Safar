Mizo Cuisine

Mizo cuisine is known for its simplicity and use of fresh ingredients. Rice is a staple, and the dishes often feature fish, meat, and seasonal vegetables.

Popular Dishes:

  • Bai

  • Sanpiau

  • Farcha

  • Ngahiong

  • Vawksa Rep

  • Koat Pitha

  • Thangjam

  • Masala Boi

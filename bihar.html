Bihari Cuisine

Bihar's cuisine is simple yet flavorful, often featuring lentils, vegetables, and rice. The food is often less spicy, highlighting the natural flavors of the ingredients.

Popular Dishes:

  • Litti Chokha

  • Sattu Paratha

  • Dal Puri

  • Kadhi Badi

  • Bihari Kebab

  • Chura Dahi

  • Khaja

About SvadSafar

Welcome to SvadSafar! A journey through the diverse and mouth-watering flavors of India. From street food to gourmet dishes, each state offers its own unique culinary heritage. Join us in celebrating the art of food where creativity, culture, and taste blend harmoniously.

About Me

Hi, I'm Pranjal Singh, a passionate full-stack developer and the creator of SvadSafar. I’m currently pursuing my undergraduate degree at JIIT Noida, India, and love working on projects that combine creativity with technology. Food and coding are two of my biggest interests, and this project is a reflection of both! Through SvadकाSafar, I hope to showcase the incredible diversity of Indian cuisines and how food connects people from different cultures.

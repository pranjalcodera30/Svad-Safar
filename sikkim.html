Sikkimese Cuisine

Sikkimese cuisine is influenced by Tibetan and Nepalese flavors. It emphasizes healthy ingredients and often features rice, vegetables, and meat.

Popular Dishes:

  • Momo

  • Thukpa

  • Phagshapa

  • Sikkimese Pickles

  • Gya Kho

  • Chhurpi

  • Sel Roti

  • Bhuteko Chana

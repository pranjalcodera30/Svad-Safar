Tripura Cuisine

Tripura's cuisine is a delightful blend of flavors and traditions, showcasing the state's rich cultural heritage. Known for its simplicity, the food of Tripura often includes rice and fish, flavored with a variety of local herbs and spices.

Popular Dishes:

  • Mui Borok

  • Bai

  • Fish Curry

  • Chakhwi

  • Wahan

  • Rohu Fish Curry

  • Peetha
